Frequently Asked Questions
How to build a website?
To build a website, start by choosing a domain name and web hosting. Then, design your site's layout and content using a website builder or by coding it yourself using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
How long will it take to get a new website?
The time it takes to build a new website can vary depending on factors such as complexity, size, customization, and the availability of resources. Typically, a simple website can be built within a few days to a couple of weeks, while more complex projects may take several weeks or even months to complete. Working closely with a web development team can help provide a more accurate estimate based on your specific requirements and timeline.
Do you only create HTML websites?
We use various technologies and platforms beyond just HTML, such as content management systems (CMS) like WordPress, e-commerce platforms like Shopify, and custom web applications using frameworks like React or Angular. Our company's goal is to deliver the most suitable solution based on the client's requirements and objectives.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Yes, ensuring that your website is mobile-friendly is a standard practice in modern web development. We prioritize responsive design, which means your website will adapt seamlessly to different screen sizes and devices, including smartphones and tablets. This ensures an optimal user experience for visitors accessing your site from various devices, enhancing usability and engagement.
Will you maintain my site for me?
Yes, we offer website maintenance services to ensure that your site remains up-to-date, secure, and optimized for performance. Our maintenance packages can include regular updates to content, software, and security features, as well as troubleshooting and technical support to address any issues that may arise. We aim to provide ongoing support to help you maximize the effectiveness and longevity of your website.
I’m on a strict budget. Do you have any low cost options?
Yes, we understand the importance of working within budget constraints. We offer various options to accommodate your budget while still delivering quality results. This may include streamlined website packages with essential features, utilizing cost-effective technologies and tools, or phased development approaches to spread costs over time. Our goal is to provide cost-effective solutions tailored to your needs without compromising on quality or functionality.
